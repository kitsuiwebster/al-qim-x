# Grammar Rules

## Phonetics (from French)

| French | Translation |
|--------|-------------|
| é      | e           |
| è      | ë           |
| ch     | x           |
| ou     | u           |
| u      | ü           |
| gu     | g           |
| j      | j           |
| y      | y           |

## General Rules

- **No uppercase letters needed**
- **All "K" sounds are written as "Q"**
- **No "R" sounds in Qimëx**
- **Plural:** Add "x" or "ex"
  - miu → miux
  - tomtom → tomtomex
- **Verb infinitive:** Add prefix "fe"

## Grammar Rules

- **Word order:** Subject-Verb-Object (like English)
- **Adjectives:** Always come AFTER the noun
  - yali gama talox (your red hands)
  - il fëm wisux (the blue eyes)
- **Verb conjugation:** Verbs don't change, use pronouns for clarity
  - wali to (I am), yali to (you are), ali to (he/she is)
- **Articles:**
  - Singular: al (the), a (a/an)
  - Plural: il (the), i (some/des)
- **Possession:** [possessor] + [object]
  - nicole son (Nicole's son)
  - samy miu (Samy's cat)
- **"Of" construction:** [object] + om + [possessor]
  - al tomtom om al casame (the roof of the house)

## Verb Patterns

- **Regular verbs:** Often end in "-om" (solom, qolom, folom)

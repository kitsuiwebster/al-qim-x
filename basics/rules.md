# Grammar Rules

## Phonetics (from French)

| French | Translation |
|--------|-------------|
| é      | e           |
| è      | ë           |
| ch     | x           |
| ou     | u           |
| u      | ü           |

## General Rules

- **No uppercase letters needed**
- **Plural:** Add "x" or "ex"
  - miu → miux
  - tomtom → tomtomex
- **Verb infinitive:** Add prefix "fe"

## Possessive Examples

- **Nicole's son:** nicole son
- **Samy's cat:** samy miu
- **The roof of the house:** al tomtom om al casame
